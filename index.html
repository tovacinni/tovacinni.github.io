I am currently working on robust retrieval using private LLMs, using a hierarchical caching system specialized for tensors and KV cache. If this sounds interesting, please reach out to towaki@outerport.com.

Until recently, I worked as a Research Scientist at NVIDIA Research on the Hyperscale Graphics Systems team with David Luebke and Morgan McGuire.

I was also concurrently a Computer Science PhD student (on leave) at the University of Toronto, supervised by Sanja Fidler and Alec Jacobson, supported by the Royal Bank of Canada, Borealis AI Fellowship.

My interests broadly span machine learning, systems, computer graphics, and computer vision. In particular, I'm interested in compression, retrieval, distribution formats, and infrastructure for multimedia. In the past, I have worked on neural fields [1] and their combination with differentiable data structures [2,3,4] as a new compressed data format for immersive media (3D JPEG!)- as well as foundation models [5] for generating them.
